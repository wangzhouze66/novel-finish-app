Use this document to build a mixed target style model for outlines, route comparison, and later style-sensitive expansion. Describe style patterns and constraints; do not copy source prose.

## Scene rhythm rules

- Placeholder for pacing rules: setup, banter, escalation, spectacle, silence, reflection, aftertaste.

## Forbidden style breaks

- Do not jump directly to full final-chapter prose before route and outline approval.
- Do not flatten the story into pure power-scaling or pure romance.
- Do not remove humor entirely from heavy routes.
- Do not over-explain every mystery if the chosen route requires open resonance.
- Do not imitate source prose verbatim.

## Rewrite rules

- Preserve emotional restraint before direct catharsis.
- Keep route-specific tone consistent with the selected ending balance.
- Check every major scene against character logic and unresolved-thread recovery.
- Prefer concrete emotional images over abstract declarations.
