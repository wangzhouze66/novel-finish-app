# Dragon Raja / Long Zu Scene-Level Outline v1

This document expands the most structurally and emotionally critical chapters from `13_chapter_level_outline_v1.md` into scene-level planning cards for `雨后王座无人 / The Empty Throne After Rain`.

It is still a **planning document**. It does **not** draft full chapters, does **not** create final prose, and does **not** change the app UI.

## 0. Planning purpose

This file exists to test whether the hybrid ending route can survive scene-level pressure before any prose drafting begins. The selected chapters form the route's emotional spine:

1. **Chapter 1. `Rain Check at Kassel`** — the opening wound chapter.
2. **Chapter 4. `Red Car, Wet City`** — the major relationship-return chapter focused on Nono's agency.
3. **Chapter 8. `The Road That Remembered`** — the truth-revelation chapter for Odin, memory, fate, and Chu Zihang's burden.
4. **Chapter 10. `The Empty Throne Protocol`** — Lu Mingfei's final route-selection chapter.
5. **Chapter 16. `Graduation After the Apocalypse`** — the final aftermath / resonance chapter.

Scene-level expansion should preserve the mixed style model from `07_author_style_bible.md`: youthful academy texture where contrast is needed, tragic emotional density, epic world-ending pressure, reflective maturity, humor before heaviness, loneliness / fate / youth / sacrifice / lost time motifs, action followed by emptiness, and grand conflicts expressed through individual choices.

Chinese planning label: `本文件只做场景级规划，不写终章正文。每一场都必须回答：这场戏推动了谁的选择，又留下了什么余味？`

# Chapter 1. Rain Check at Kassel

## Chapter selection card

1. **Why this chapter was selected:** It is the opening wound chapter. The ending cannot earn epic stakes unless it first reminds the reader what ordinary youth, school comedy, embarrassment, cafeteria noise, and classroom routine are supposed to protect.
2. **Chapter function in the final volume:** Reintroduce Kassel as a living academy while showing that memory-erasure damage and succession pressure have infected ordinary systems.
3. **Emotional target:** Familiar warmth slowly contaminated by absence; the reader should smile first, then realize the smile has been placed beside a memorial.
4. **Main unresolved thread addressed:** MR-01, MR-04, SR-05, ET-01, ET-05, WT-05.
5. **Main character arc movement:** Lu Mingfei starts by wanting to hide inside comedy and ends by choosing not to look away from the wrongness.
6. **Style risk:** Too much lore too early would flatten the wound; too much comedy would make the final-volume promise feel delayed.
7. **Scene list:** Four scenes, moving from campus arrival to cafeteria banter, classroom anomaly, and private recognition.

## Scene 1.1 — Placeholder title: `Late Arrival in the Rain`

1. **Scene number:** 1.1
2. **Scene title placeholder:** `Late Arrival in the Rain`
3. **Scene location:** Kassel College gate, wet path toward dormitory and classroom district.
4. **Characters present:** Lu Mingfei, gate staff, passing students, unnamed maintenance crew.
5. **Scene purpose:** Reopen academy texture through ordinary inconvenience before the plot wound appears.
6. **Plot movement:** Lu Mingfei arrives late with soaked luggage; campus repairs and casual student movement establish a post-crisis normality that feels almost believable.
7. **Emotional subtext:** Lu Mingfei wants the school to laugh at him because ridicule would prove the world is still normal.
8. **Character decision or avoidance:** He avoids asking why the campus feels rehearsed and chooses a comic complaint instead.
9. **Relationship movement:** Minor students treat him as familiar but slightly displaced, preparing the later empty-seat unease.
10. **Conflict type:** Environment vs. ordinary dignity; surface comedy vs. hidden dread.
11. **Dialogue function:** Light banter should reestablish his loser-comedy register and create contrast for later silence.
12. **Action / silence balance:** 70% movement and comic business, 20% dialogue, 10% brief pauses at odd campus details.
13. **Symbolic image:** A wet suitcase wheel stuck in a crack where rainwater reflects a campus light.
14. **Atmosphere notes:** Rain should feel inconvenient, not apocalyptic yet; the wrongness hides inside maintenance tarps, repaired glass, and too-clean announcements.
15. **Foreshadowing payoff or setup:** Sets up rain, late-arrival inversion, repaired campus, and ordinary detail after future apocalypse.
16. **Worldbuilding function:** Shows Kassel's emergency repairs and subtle archive-system instability without explaining the mechanism.
17. **Style strategy from `07_author_style_bible.md`:** Begin with youthful academy tone, concrete everyday detail, and humor before heaviness.
18. **Risk if mishandled:** Opening with ominous prophecy or lore will lose the necessary school-life contrast.
19. **Transition to next scene:** Lu Mingfei follows food noise and student gossip toward the cafeteria.
20. **Revision target for later drafting:** Make the first page answer, “What ordinary thing is being protected?”

# Chapter 4. Red Car, Wet City

## Chapter selection card

1. **Why this chapter was selected:** It is the major relationship-return chapter and the clearest early test of whether Nono remains a free actor rather than a reward, guide, or romantic device.
2. **Chapter function in the final volume:** Reposition Nono as someone who chooses responsibility and movement on her own terms while helping secure access, evacuation, and witness routes.
3. **Emotional target:** Flirtatious velocity that becomes painful adult ambiguity: care is real because it cannot be owned.
4. **Main unresolved thread addressed:** SR-05, CA-05, CA-09, WT-06, ET-05.
5. **Main character arc movement:** Lu Mingfei learns not to demand certainty from Nono; Nono shows care through decisive action without surrendering freedom.
6. **Style risk:** Nono could become a romantic reward, plot taxi, or evasive stereotype.
7. **Scene list:** Four scenes, moving from interruption, wet-city pursuit, boundary conversation, and chosen return.

# Chapter 8. The Road That Remembered

1. **Why this chapter was selected:** It is the truth-revelation chapter that turns Odin from abstract dread into an operational threat without stripping away mythic terror.
2. **Chapter function in the final volume:** Define the memory/fate rule behind the rain road and prepare Chapter 12's defeat condition.
3. **Emotional target:** Confirmation without comfort; Chu receives truth as a heavier burden, not healing.
4. **Main unresolved thread addressed:** MR-03, MR-04, CA-03, CA-08, WT-03, MT-03, MT-04.

6. **Style risk:** Too much Odin explanation humanizes the dread; too little clarity makes later victory arbitrary.
7. **Scene list:** Four scenes, moving from monitored entry to loop, witness resistance, and burdened breakthrough.
