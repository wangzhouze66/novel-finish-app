# Character Evidence

## Purpose

Collect repeated evidence for character behavior, emotional logic, relationship movement, contradictions, and ending implications.

## Review Questions

- What does the character choose under pressure?
- What does the character avoid saying directly?
- Which relationships repeatedly define the character's identity?
- Which contradiction must an ending resolve, transform, or preserve intentionally?
